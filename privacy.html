Privacy Policy for U2Them

Last updated: June 2026

U2Them ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use U2Them, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, username, and password (securely handled via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile information: bio, profile picture, and gallery photos you choose to upload
  • Chat messages and data you send within the app
  • Friend list and friend request data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate chat, profile, and friend features
  • Allow you to connect and communicate with other users
  • Display your profile and shared content to your friends
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

All user data is securely stored using Google Firebase services. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Advertising

U2Them may display ads through Google AdMob to support free use of the app. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as required to operate core app features (such as Firebase for authentication and storage) or as required by law.

6. Your Choices

You can edit or delete your profile information, photos, and account at any time within the app. You may also contact us to request account deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

U2Them is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
